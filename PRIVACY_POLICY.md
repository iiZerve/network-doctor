# Privacy Policy for Network Doctor

**Last updated:** June 2026

Network Doctor ("we", "us", or "our") operates the Network Doctor mobile application (the "App").

## Information Collection and Use

The App is designed to perform local network diagnostics on your device. It does **not** collect, store, or transmit any personal information or device identifiers to us or any third party.

- All network checks (ping timing, DNS lookups via public DoH, connectivity tests, packet loss measurement) are performed directly from your device to public infrastructure (e.g., 8.8.8.8, 1.1.1.1, Cloudflare, Google).
- Results are processed and displayed only locally on your device.
- The "background monitoring" feature runs network probes at intervals you control (default is very infrequent in "Light" mode) and stores results only locally.
- No data leaves your device except the standard network requests necessary for the diagnostics you initiate.

The app's user interface is built with standard web technologies and loads its rendering libraries (Tailwind CSS, Chart.js, and Font Awesome icons) from public Content Delivery Networks (CDNs) on first launch. These requests are made solely to fetch UI assets and contain no diagnostic data, user identifiers, or personal information. No analytics, tracking pixels, or third-party advertising SDKs are used.

We do not use cookies for tracking, analytics, advertising SDKs, or any third-party services that track you beyond the minimal CDN requests for UI framework files.

## Log Data

If the App crashes, standard Android crash logs (which may include device model, OS version, and stack traces) may be generated by the system. These are not sent to us unless you explicitly share them (e.g., via email for support).

## Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted in the App or on the GitHub repository.

## Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us via the GitHub repository for the project (iiZerve/network-doctor) or the email associated with the developer account.

This App is provided "as is" for diagnostic purposes. Always verify network issues with other tools and your ISP when necessary.
